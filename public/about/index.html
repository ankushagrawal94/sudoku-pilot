Learn and practice Sudoku

Sudoku coach

Get Help With the Next Sudoku Move

Open the coach when you get stuck. Sudoku Pilot finds the logical moves available on the current board and shows the evidence for each one.

 Step-by-step hints

Sudoku Hints That Explain the Reasoning

Sudoku Pilot checks your board and notes, then gives the hint in four clues: the technique, a candidate, the search area, and the exact move. Stop as soon as you have enough information to continue.

 Technique practice

Skip the Boring Part of Sudoku Practice

Select the Sudoku techniques you already know and run them automatically. You can spend more of the puzzle looking for techniques you are still learning, without asking the app to reveal which one comes next.

 Candidate entry

Enter Sudoku Candidate Notes Faster

Sudoku Pilot can fill every legal candidate, add one candidate to several cells, or leave all note entry to you. Choose the amount of help you want for the current puzzle.

 Configurable controls

Change Sudoku Input and Highlight Settings

Open Preferences to change how numbers are entered and what the board highlights. Each setting is saved in your browser.

Use Sudoku Pilot your way

Screenshot import

Import a Sudoku Puzzle From a Screenshot

Upload a screenshot of one Sudoku grid. Sudoku Pilot drafts the filled digits and pencil notes in an editable review screen. Check every cell, fix any OCR errors, and start solving.

 Offline Sudoku

Use Sudoku Pilot Offline

Sudoku Pilot is a Progressive Web App. Open it once with a connection, then install it from your browser for an app-like icon and offline access.

 Quiet, local play

Play Sudoku Without Ads or an Account

Sudoku Pilot is free. There are no ads, subscriptions, paid hints, lives, token packs, or account prompts. The current app has no monetization layer.

 Player-controlled feedback

Turn Sudoku Mistake Warnings On or Off

Sudoku Pilot starts with live mistake warnings turned off. You can test an idea, catch your own error, and continue without losing a life. Turn warnings on in Preferences if you want immediate feedback.

How the app was built

Our puzzle guarantee

Every Generated Puzzle Can Be Solved Without Guessing

Every puzzle generated by Sudoku Pilot has one solution and a complete logical path. The app solves the puzzle with its own teaching techniques before adding it to the game.

 Puzzle variety

How Sudoku Pilot Finds Genuinely Different Puzzles

Sudoku Pilot treats digit swaps, row and column rearrangements, rotations, reflections, and transpositions as versions of the same puzzle. The catalog keeps one canonical identity for that whole family.

 Puzzle difficulty

How We Rate Sudoku Difficulty and Find Better Puzzles

Sudoku Pilot rates each generated puzzle by solving it. Expert and Extreme puzzles need at least five separate points where easier techniques run out and a move from that level is required.

 Why we built this

Why We Built Sudoku Pilot

I kept reading about techniques such as X-Wings and Skyscrapers. Then I would open a puzzle and fail to see them. I built Sudoku Pilot to get more practice with that part.
