Play Sudoku Without Ads or an Account

Sudoku Pilot is free. There are no ads, subscriptions, paid hints, lives, token packs, or account prompts. The current app has no monetization layer.

By Admin · Updated July 12, 2026

The coach is part of the free app

Puzzle generation, candidate tools, every hint level, technique automation, and focused practice are available without a payment or an ad view.

There is no token balance, daily hint limit, premium technique list, or mistake counter that ends a game.

Hints open without an ad break

The technique clue and its full explanation are next to each other. You can move between them without waiting for a video or closing a promotion.

There are no reward videos, streaks, or lives. Close the app when you are done and return to the saved board later.

No account is required

The current grid, pencil notes, preferences, and undo history live in local browser storage. Closing the tab keeps your progress on that device.

There is no account backend or cross-device sync. Clear local data in the More panel removes the saved puzzle and preferences from the current browser.

Screenshot recognition stays on your device

Screenshot import runs in the browser. The selected image is not sent to an OCR service.
